The Big Five of Psychology are five traits used to describe human personalities.

The five traits are:
Openness
Conscientiousness
Extraversion
Agreeableness
Neuroticism

Using these traits, and data on a person's:
Age
Gender
Race
Country
as well as many other characteristics, it is possible to make many interesting insights into trends in personality.

From: http://personality-testing.info/_rawdata/

  • Areas of questions

    For each of the five traits, we formed a simple hypothesis:

  • Agreeableness

    Older people have higher agreeableness scores

  • Neuroticism

    Non-native English speakers in the USA have higher neuroticism scores

  • Conscientiousness

    People who are left-handed have higher conscientiousness scores

  • Extraversion

    Australians have higher extraversion scores

  • Openness

    People of mixed race have higher openness scores
